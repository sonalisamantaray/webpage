Wodonga Park Are True Belivers

In Natural And Organic Nourishment!

We Believe In The Old Adage:"you are what you eat"

The same goes for plants: to be nutritious the right balance of natural fertilisers is used and our skilled team apply biodynamic preparations and natural remedies to enhance the quality of the product. Biodiversity within the farming environment limits the damage of insect attack – which are also controlled with natural preparations and the release of predators such as lady beetles and wasps. Synthetic herbicides and fertilisers are not used – instead nature is emulated and by mowing, the grass is recycled and returned to the soil. Fertility is further increased by adding rock-dust and other natural fertilisers such as compost, fish, and kelp emulsion. Nature cannot be perfected upon.
